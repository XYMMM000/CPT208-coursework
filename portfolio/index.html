“Build, Test, Share, Climb”

ClimbQuest tells one complete climbing story in six scenes. Scroll like a game timeline: create your own route, test and customize it, evaluate difficulty, publish to community, and improve through feedback.

CLIMBQUEST

Step 01 · Create

DIY Route Builder

Users freely create climbing routes by choosing hold sequence, movement rhythm, and target style.

CreateTestRateShare

Step 02 · Test

Ability Test & Route Customization

Users run a quick performance test, then the system tunes route intensity and recommends personalized adjustments.

Why This Matters

Beginners avoid random trial-and-error and get routes matched to confidence, not just raw grade.

Step 03 · Rate

Route Difficulty Evaluation

Creators evaluate their own route with a clear level tag, making challenge intensity transparent for everyone.

Feedback Anchor

Difficulty labels create a shared language for comparison, coaching, and safer progression.

Step 04 · Share

Publish to Web App

Routes are shared to the platform so other climbers can try the same challenge.

Step 05 · Community Review

Peer Ratings & Comments

Other users rate quality, difficulty consistency, and climbing experience.

Step 06 · Improve

Iterate the Route

Creators refine hold sequence and grading from real-world feedback loops.
