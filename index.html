CPT208 Coursework Project · C2 Go Climbers

A playful onsite mobile system for climbers and coaches

ClimbQuest is a human-centred web-based climbing experience designed to support route discovery, progress tracking, coaching feedback, and social motivation in climbing gyms.

Project Snapshot

Problem Statement

Why does this project matter?

Many climbers, especially beginners, struggle to decide which routes to try, how to track improvement, and how to receive feedback in a supportive way. At the same time, coaches and experienced climbers often lack a simple interface to guide and motivate learners during onsite sessions.

ClimbQuest addresses this gap by transforming the climbing gym experience into a playful, structured, and socially engaging journey.

Target Users

Who are we designing for?

Primary Persona

Name: Amy, 21, beginner climber

Amy has recently started indoor climbing. She feels excited but often confused about route difficulty, training goals, and how to improve after failed attempts.

Secondary Persona

Name: Jason, 28, climbing coach

Jason wants a lightweight digital tool to recommend routes, leave quick feedback, and keep climbers motivated without interrupting the natural flow of onsite practice.

Core Playful Features

What makes ClimbQuest engaging?

1. Route Quest

Users receive personalised climbing tasks based on level, confidence, and session goals, turning climbing into a guided challenge journey.

2. Progress Badges

Achievements, streaks, and milestone badges encourage persistence, celebrate improvement, and visualise personal growth over time.

3. Coach Feedback

Coaches can provide simple, quick, supportive feedback cards that help climbers reflect on technique and next-step improvements.
