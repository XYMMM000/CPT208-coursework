AI Disclosure and Acknowledgement

In accordance with the CPT208 course policy on artificial intelligence, the following disclosure is provided:

AI Tools Used:

  • Claude 3.5 Sonnet (v1.0) - For code scaffolding, writing refinement, and ideation brainstorming
  • GitHub Copilot (v0.x) - For frontend development assistance and debugging

Usage Scope (Substantive Use, as permitted):

  • AI was used to generate initial HTML/CSS templates and helper functions during frontend prototyping
  • Prompts were carefully designed to ensure that the generated code was reviewed, tested, and modified to align with specific user requirements identified through our research
  • All core design logic, user flow decisions, and requirement validation were performed autonomously by the team

Verification and Testing:

  • All AI-generated code was manually reviewed and tested against user requirements (See Prototype page for example)
  • Design decisions were grounded in user research findings, not AI suggestions
  • Usability testing confirmed that the system met the three must-have requirements without relying on AI-derived assumptions

Ethical Considerations:

  • We ensured that the use of AI did not compromise accessibility, inclusivity, or human-centred design principles
  • The system was designed with care to avoid bias or exclusion, values that transcend AI capabilities
  • AI tools were treated as productivity aids, not as design decision-makers
